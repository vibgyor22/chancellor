Chancellor is a flagship brand from the house of S M & Sons — Manufacturers, Suppliers and Exporters of premium handkerchiefs, towels and socks. For decades, we have delivered excellence in fabric and finish from the heart of New Delhi.

Our exhaustive range — from intricately embroidered handkerchiefs and monogrammed cotton squares, to plush bath towels and refined socks — is crafted using top-quality fabrics under the meticulous supervision of seasoned experts.
